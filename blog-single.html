5 Things to Look for When Buying a Wallet

  • 24 APR 2019
  • Prague Kalaivana, Marketing & Fashion
  • LIFE HACKS, HEALTH, FASHION

Everyone needs a wallet and we all have bought and owned at least one wallet in our life. But do you really know what to look for when buying a wallet? Here are the top 5 things.

1. How thin is it?

Thick, fat wallets over time can lead to a number of problems from bulging jeans that make you look bad to back pain and sciatica. So always see how thin a wallet is when you fill it up. One way of knowing this is by checking for nylon microfiber. If the entire wallet or at least the inside of the wallet is made from nylon microfiber you can be assured that it is going to be up to 50% thinner than regular wallets. Further nylon Microfiber helps in making the wallets more durable so they last longer.


2. How heavy is it?

You donâ€™t want to be carrying a wallet that makes your pants sag or puts on a few extra pounds. So always look at how heavy a wallet is to being with. One thing to keep in mind is again is that nylon microfiber weighs much less than regular materials making wallets over 50% lighter.

3. Are the card pockets big enough?

Not all cards are the same size, some tend to be thicker, some longer and some heavier. You need to find a wallet with card slots that are wide enough to fit all types of cards while not making it a battle to take them out every single time.

4. Is the inside slippery?

70% of times when you lose something in your wallet, it is because the inside of the wallet was slippery and the card or cash fell out because of it. So when you get a wallet remember to check for slip-proof interiors that also allow you to take them out easily.

Your wallet can affect your everyday quality of life: your comfort from the weight and thickness you carry with you, to how you access and spend (or not spend) your money, or even your posture and health!

5. Is it easy to maintain and keep clean?

After a few years or even months, wallets rip around the edges or tear on the inside. Further, it is a hassle to keep clean and no one has time for that. To avoid this, find a wallet that water resistant and machine washable. If it can survive the washing machine it can survive a lot of years in your pocket. Ask for to find out if the wallet has a double-edged perimeter stitching, as it makes a wallet sturdier and last for over 4 years.
